To enable/disabel OpenGL "Sync to VBlank" from xwindows/xfce4:

1)  Click on "Apps" tool bar at top of screen

2)  Choose "Settings" on pull down menu

3)  Choose NVIDIA XServer Settings

4)  From NVIDIA Settings window:

   a - click on OpenGL Settings

   b - enable/disable with "Sync to VBank" 

When checked, the max fps would be 60.  For example zig plotting, it is possible to acheive 1600 fps.
